The Birth of Our Metaverse English Club

In 2024, I co-founded a club that quickly became one of my favorite projects: the Metaverse English Research Club. What makes this club special is its commitment to creating a fun, interactive, and immersive learning environment. We wanted to break the fear often associated with learning English, replacing it with curiosity and joy.

Our approach is playful and innovative. Through gamified teaching, virtual reality (VR) experiences, and even board games, members engage with English in ways that feel natural, tangible, and exciting. Every session is designed so that learning feels less like a task and more like an adventure.

Working alongside Joseph, we’ve turned the club into more than just a language-learning space. It’s a mini technological lab, a place where creativity meets education, and a community built with care and passion. Members don’t just improve their English—they experience it, practice it, and enjoy it in a supportive, vibrant environment.

No matter your current level of English, the club offers a chance to discover the joy of learning, and to grow continuously in a relaxed and inspiring atmosphere. It’s a small experiment in blending technology, education, and human connection—but one that has left a lasting mark on everyone involved.
For more information, visit our club website: Metaverse English Research Club
